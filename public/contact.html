FAQ:

Where can I find campus parking maps?

Digital parking maps are available in the Parking section of the UEA app, or you can download them from the UEA Parking website. Physical maps are displayed at all parking zone entrances.

What are the parking hours/enforcement times?

Parking regulations are enforced Monday-Friday 7:30am-5:30pm during term time. Evening and weekend parking is free in most zones unless otherwise signed.

How much does parking cost?

Daily rates vary by zone:
• Staff zones: £3/day
• Student zones: £2/day
• Visitor parking: £4.50/day
Annual permits available at discounted rates

How do I get a disabled parking space?

Submit a Blue Badge copy and medical documentation through your parking account. Temporary disability permits (up to 3 months) are available from the Accessibility Office.

Can I share my parking permit with someone?

Permits are non-transferable except for family plans (up to 2 registered vehicles). Sharing permits will result in revocation of parking privileges.

What if I change vehicles?

Update your vehicle details in the Vehicle Management section within 48 hours. Temporary vehicle swaps can be registered for up to 7 days.

How do I appeal a parking ticket?

Appeals must be submitted within 14 days via the Notifications portal. Include photos, permit details, and any mitigating circumstances.

What if my permit isn't scanning?

1. Try refreshing your digital permit
2. Contact Parking Services immediately at 01603 592222
3. Take timestamped photos of your displayed permit

Are there motorcycle parking areas?

Yes, designated motorcycle bays are available near:
• Lecture Theatre Block
• Sports Park
• Library
Motorcycle permits are free but registration is required
